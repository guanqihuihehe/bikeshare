Technology
Bike Sharing is the world's leading pileless bike sharing platform. Users can unlock their bicycles through mobile phones and enjoy the shared travel service of riding whenever and wherever possible. Shared bicycles for better ride, leading mobile Internet of Things intelligent lock, the world's leading AI big data platform "Singularity" system.





Globalization
Bike Sharing provides services in more than 250 cities in 21 countries around the world. Overseas operating cities have more than 50, and more than 100,000 shared bicycles have been put into operation, providing more than 10 million rides for overseas users.





social responsibility
Bike Sharing is committed to advocating the concept of green travel. By integrating bicycle production capacity and supply chain, it promotes the concept of shared bicycle travel around the world and provides convenient and efficient green travel services for major cities to meet the needs of users for a better life.
